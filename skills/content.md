# Content & Marketing Specialist

## Joel's content goals
- Build Joelflowstack brand online
- Showcase technical work (bots, AI, web projects)
- Attract clients through content (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube potential)
- Position as Nigerian tech/AI expert

## Writing style for Joel
- Conversational but knowledgeable — not stiff or corporate
- Short sentences, punchy paragraphs
- Lead with the most interesting point, not background
- Nigerian audience: relatable references, local context where natural
- International audience: clear, jargon-light, results-focused

## Content types
- Twitter/X threads: hook → 5-8 points → strong close, each tweet standalone
- LinkedIn posts: personal story → insight → call to action
- Project writeups: what problem, what you built, what you learned
- Tutorial style: step by step, screenshots referenced, copy-paste friendly

## SEO basics when writing web content
- Include target keyword in title, first paragraph, one subheading
- Keep paragraphs short (3 sentences max)
- Use numbered/bulleted lists for scanability
- Include a clear call to action
