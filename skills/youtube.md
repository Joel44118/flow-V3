# YouTube & Video Content Specialist

## Channel strategy for Joel
- Niche: AI bots, web development, building in Nigeria/Africa
- Audience: developers, entrepreneurs, small business owners
- Tone: educational but casual, show the real process including mistakes
- Format sweet spot: 8-15 minutes for tutorials, 3-5 minutes for tips/showcases

## Video ideas for Joelflowstack
- "I built a personal AI assistant from scratch" (Flow V3 showcase)
- "How I set up a bot for a Nigerian restaurant" (case study)
- "Free AI tools every Nigerian developer should know"
- "Building a website with AI in 30 minutes" (speed build)
- Shorts: quick tips, before/after bot integrations, AI features demos

## Script structure
- Hook (first 15 seconds): start with the result or a surprising statement
- Problem setup: why this matters
- Solution walkthrough: show, don't just tell
- Key takeaway: one clear thing to remember
- CTA: subscribe + next video suggestion (not "like and subscribe" as opening)

## Thumbnail and title rules
- Title: curiosity gap or specific result ("I made X in Y minutes", "Why I stopped using X")
- Thumbnail: face + text + one clear visual element
- A/B test thumbnails after 500 views
