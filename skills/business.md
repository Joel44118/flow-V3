## When advising on business
- Be specific and actionable — no generic advice
- Consider the Nigerian market context (mobile-first users, WhatsApp dominant, price sensitivity)
- Fiverr/Upwork strategy is valid for international clients
- Local outreach matters for Nigerian clients — WhatsApp, Instagram, LinkedIn

## Joelflowstack positioning
- Differentiate on: AI-powered bots, personal touch, technical depth
- Bot integration is underserved in Nigerian SME market — lean into this
- Portfolio pieces matter more than certificates at this stage
- Case studies from real projects (even personal ones) build credibility

## Growth priorities
- Build in public — document Flow V3 development as portfolio content
